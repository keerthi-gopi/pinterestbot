PinBot

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how PinBot ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and handles information when you use our Telegram bot and related services.

1. Information We Collect

When you use PinBot, we may collect the following:

  • Telegram user ID and username (to process your requests)
  • Amazon product URLs you send to the bot
  • Publicly available product data scraped from Amazon

2. How We Use Information

We use the information solely to:

  • Scrape Amazon product data from URLs you provide
  • Generate Pinterest pin content using AI
  • Post generated pins to your connected Telegram channel
  • Post generated pins to your connected Pinterest board

3. Pinterest Data

PinBot uses the Pinterest API to create and publish pins on your behalf. We request only the minimum permissions required: pins:write and boards:read. We do not store your Pinterest credentials or access tokens beyond what is needed to operate the service.

4. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data, product URLs, or generated content in a database. All processing happens in real time and is discarded after the request is complete.

5. Third Party Services

PinBot uses the following third party services:

  • Telegram API — to receive and send messages
  • Scrapingdog — to scrape Amazon product data
  • Google Gemini API — to generate pin content
  • Pinterest API — to publish pins

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties except as required to operate the services listed above.

7. Security

All API keys and credentials are stored securely as environment variables and are never exposed publicly.

8. Children's Privacy

PinBot is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
